ticket-purchase:
The ticket purchase page shows the details of the tickets that the user are
purchasing on left in the form of a table, on the right is a form
where the user enters their payment information, and under the form
they can see the total amount and breakdown of costs.

upon clicking the purchase, the website database will process the
purchase then redirect to the ticket purchase confirm page

ticket-purchase-confirm:
The ticket purchase confirm page shows a breakdown of tickets on
the left in teh form of a table, and on the right is a QR code
that will be shown at the event to allow access. below the qr code
is a breakdown of the costs of the ticket/s
